详细描述

Subclass of RMAbstractMercatorWebSource for access to OGC WMS Server.

Example: RMWMS *wms = [[RMWMS alloc] init]; [wms setUrlPrefix:"http://vmap0.tiles.osgeo.org/wms/vmap0"]; [wms setLayers:@"basic"]; RMWMSSource *wmsSource = [[RMWMSSource alloc] init]; [wmsSource setWms:wms]; [mapContents setTileSource:wmsSource]; [wmsSource release]; [wms release];
